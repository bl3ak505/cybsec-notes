# 📌 CEH V13 — Free Study Resources

> A curated list of free resources for CEH v13 (312-50v13) prep — covering theory, practice questions, hands-on labs, cheat sheets, and communities.

## 🔍 What's New in V13

- **AI-driven attack modules** — includes AI-assisted hacking techniques (some labs require ShellGPT/ChatGPT)
- **Cloud security** — AWS, Azure, GCP coverage expanded
- **IoT/OT security** — more weight than v12
- **20 domains**, 125 MCQs, 4-hour exam (312-50v13)

## 🧠 Core Concepts

- **Exam code** — 312-50v13
- **Format** — 125 multiple-choice questions, 4 hours
- **Passing score** — 70% (varies slightly by form)
- **Domains** — Footprinting, Scanning, Enumeration, Vulnerability Analysis, System Hacking, Malware, Sniffing, Social Engineering, DoS, Session Hijacking, Evading IDS/Firewalls, Web Servers, Web Apps, SQLi, Hacking Wireless/Mobile, IoT/OT, Cloud, Cryptography, Pen Testing

## 🔥 Important Details & Gotchas

- **V13 added AI labs** — some require a ChatGPT/ShellGPT subscription to complete. Theory portion is still free to study.
- **80% of exam questions come from official EC-Council courseware** — if you can get access to the 4-volume official material (via library, institution, or otherwise), prioritize it.
- **ExamTopics ≠ braindump** — the community corrections are more valuable than the questions themselves. Always read the comments.
- **Don't memorize, understand** — CEH loves trick answers where two choices look nearly identical. Understanding *why* saves you every time.
- **V13 delisted some older labs** — the cavementech guide marks deleted labs in red, still worth knowing them for the theory.
